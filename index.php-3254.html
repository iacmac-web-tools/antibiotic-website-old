Visas


Any travel agency in your country, can help you with this. For opening visa an invitation letter and service voucher from travel company are necessary. All these documents are to be represented to the Russian Embassy or Consulate.

You can contact the official conference travel agency is «VOYAGE» they will be glad to help you.

Necessary information for travel visa:

  • fax copy of travel document (passport)
  • date of arrival/departing to Russia
  • cities of visiting in Russia
  • names of hotels you stay in Russia
