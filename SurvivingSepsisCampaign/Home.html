About The Surviving Sepsis Campaign

Sepsis and septic shock are leading causes of death worldwide. Implement the Surviving Sepsis Campaign guidelines and the Hour-1 Bundle and be part of the international effort to reduce mortality and morbidity. Guidance for children is now also available. 

Read More

  • Identify Sepsis Early On Your Ward
    Clinicians on hospital wards have a role in saving lives through early sepsis identification. Download our guide implement the hour-1 bundle.
  • SSC Listserv
    The Campaign's listserv provides an active forum for professionals to share experiences and ask questions. Join SSC Listserv
  • Patients and Families
    Resources for patients and families include MyICUCare.org and the ESICM Patient and Family website.
