New SIGN decision support app

SIGN has a new app to provide a range of decision support toolkits based on our guidelines.

The first decision support tool,В COVID-19 assessment in primary care - predicting severe disease, is available now. It provides access to:

  • The rapid evidence review by SIGN and the University of Glasgow on symptoms, comorbidities, other risk factors and clinical signs in adults which may indicate a higher risk of progression to severe disease in COVID-19
  • The Scottish Government's COVID-19 Primary Care Hub Triage Guide.
