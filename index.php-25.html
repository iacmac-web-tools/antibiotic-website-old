About ANTIBIOTIC.ru

Published: Wednesday, January 01, 2003 - 00:00
Topic: Documents 

The web-site for medical professionals «Antibiotics and Antimicrobial Therapy» (ANTIBIOTIC.ru) was founded under the auspices of the Institute of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IAC) of the Smolensk State Medical Academy (SSMA), Scientific Research Center of Monitoring of Antimicrobial Resistance of Ministry of Health of Russian Federation and Interregional Association for Clinical Microbiology and Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IACMAC).

Web-site www.antibiotic.ru is designed to cover the problems of antimicrobial resistance in Russia, antimicrobials use, clinical and molecular microbiology. Also on www.antibiotic.ru the information about the activities of the Russian branch of Cochrane Collaboration and the Interregional Society of Pharmacoepidemiological Researches (ISPR) is placed.

The main principles of informational policy
of the web-site «Antibiotics and Antimicrobial Therapy»

  • All informational materials, placed on ANTIBIOTIC.ru, are open for general use on free-of-charge basis
  • All data are based on the materials of the foremost medical publications (Russian and foreign), and also on the results of the original researches directed by the Institute of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IAC), Department of Clinical Pharmacology of the Smolensk State Medical Academy, Scientific Center of the Ministry of Health of Russia of Monitoring Antimicrobial Resistance, the Interregional Association for Clinical Microbiology and Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (IACMAC) according to the principles of good microbiology practice (GMP) and evidence-based medicine (EBM)
  • The copyright of the original publications is respected. We always give references to the original sources of the information and place the publications only from the sanction of the owner of the copyright.
  • We are always open for any constructive remarks and offers from physicians, scientists and all interested individuals.
  • Advertising policy: we are funding our site ourselves. We do not display ANY advertisements on our pages, and the web-site ANTIBIOTIC.ru does not host and receive funding from advertising.
