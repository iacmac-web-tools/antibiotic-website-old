Testing and Commisioning Engineers

Engineers for Energy Automation & Industrial Automation projects: Technitab provides Electro-Mechanical Automation testing and commissioning engineer Resources contracting Automation services, designing of panel boards and labels, erection, testing and commissioning engineer, operation, maintenance services along with manpower / material supplies. Our focus is to provide better quality at competitive pricing for Energy Automation & Industrial Automation.

Technitab, India started as an IT company and now Technitab is growing as a contracting and trading company with an initiative born out of vast experience of performance driven professionals in the field of Electrical and Electro-mechanical domains. The wide exposure to energy automation, project management, installation and commissioning, operations and maintenance of the various electrical systems and equipment, as established by these professionals, provide a good technical platform to our company.

Technitab offers the following services to the electro-mechanical industry:

  • Design and Manufacturing of LT/HT and C& R panels
  • Manpower supply for testing and commissioning (Engineers & Skilled Technicians)
  • Electro-mechanical works at Substations and Processing Plants
  • Supply of materials and components as required by the projects
