OmniMedicalSearch was launched in 2004 to provide users with easy access to authoritative medical content. While the service is no longer available, users can continue to locate credible medical information on OpenMD.com, a health search engine and directory that indexes information from hundreds of reputable medical publishers, journals, and government health sites.
