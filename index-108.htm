Financial Implications of COVID-19 on Hospitals and Health Systems

The AHA has rightly labeled this pandemic the greatest financial threat in history for hospitals and health systems as we continue to fight this pandemic at the same time that non-COVID patient visits remain below normal levels. The AHA has a number of resources and reports on the impact of COVID-19 on hospitals and health systems.
