Posters from 18th European Congress of Clinical Microbiology and Infectious Diseases (ECCMID)
(19-22 April 2008, Barselona, Spain):

  • Modern informational technologies in physicians’ education in the field of socially most important diseases in Russia and beyond

    I.V. Andreeva, O.U. Stetsiouk, Y.A. Belkova, V.A. Kostin

    E-Learning (so-called distance education — DE) is a totally new, progressive way of education. This type of education is based on the idea of a student's selfdependent interactive work and on the maximal use of the modern informational technologies, i.e. PC, e-mail, Internet.

  • Analytical study of infection control measures in Russian ICUs: lessons from the real life

    R. Kozlov, D. Galkin, A. Fokin, V. Mischenko, MIX-07 Study Group

    Epidemiological assumptions show that 2-2.5 millions of patients acquire healthcare-associated infections (HAI) in Russia annually. To uncover reasons for the high prevalence we aimed to assess common practices of infection control (IC) in Russian ICUs.

    Download PDF (248 KB)
  • Assessment of carbapenem resistance and presence of acquired carbapenemases in Russian nosocomian isolates of Acinetobacter spp.

    A.A. Martinovich, O.V. Shevchenko, M.V. Edelstein, R.S. Kozlov

    Resistance to carbapenems remains rare among nosocomial Acinetobacters in Russia. However, the identification of acquired OXA-type carbapenemases in isolates from geographically distant regions of Russia poses the risk of future dissemination of resistance.
