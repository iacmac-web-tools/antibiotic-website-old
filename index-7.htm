About ISAC

The International Society of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy (ISAC) is a federation of affiliated Member Societies which aims to increase the knowledge of antimicrobial chemotherapy and combat antibiotic resistance around the world.

Learn More About Us

ISAC update on COVID-19 pandemic

ISAC is deeply aware and appreciative of the massive efforts currently being undertaken by our Member Societies and global colleagues in fighting the SARS CoV-2 / COVID-19 pandemic.

Read More

Mission Statement

Creating sustainable meta-competence in the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of infectious diseases and control of antimicrobial resistance in order to improve global health.

Member Societies
